How do search engines work?

  1. Crawlers go to websites that the know about, either from a link from another site or becaise the site has been submitted to the search engine
  2. The crawlers first look for information in the meta tags, so it is good to make sure you include helpful information there.
  3. They then check the title and any links from the page. They also visit any child pages of the website.
  4. They then check the last updated time, to check that the information is up-to-date and current. This may matter more for particular types of websites.
